1.1 SQUAT (Barbell Back Squat)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Category: Lower Body / Compound Movement
Primary Muscles: Quadriceps, Glutes, Hamstrings, Core
Equipment: Barbell, Squat Rack
Difficulty: Intermediate

PROPER FORM:
1. Posisikan barbell di upper traps (bukan di leher!)
2. Posisi kaki selebar bahu, jari kaki sedikit mengarah ke luar (15-30°)
3. Jaga dada tetap tegak, pandangan lurus ke depan atau sedikit ke atas
4. Turunkan tubuh dengan menggerakkan pinggul ke belakang (seperti duduk)
5. Turun sampai paha sejajar atau di bawah sejajar dengan lantai
6. Dorong melalui tumit saat naik ke posisi awal

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Tumit terangkat dari lantai (indicasi mobility issue)
- Lutut collapse ke dalam (weak glutes/abductors)
- Punggung membungkuk (weak core)
- Depth tidak cukup (partial rep)

ALTERNATIVES:
- Goblet Squat (beginner friendly)
- Front Squat (more quad emphasis)
- Leg Press (machine alternative)
- Bulgarian Split Squat (unilateral)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.2 BENCH PRESS (Barbell Bench Press)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Category: Upper Body / Compound Movement
Primary Muscles: Pectoralis Major, Anterior Deltoid, Triceps
Equipment: Barbell, Flat Bench
Difficulty: Intermediate

PROPER FORM:
1. Berbaring telentang di bangku, mata tepat di bawah barbell
2. Pegang barbell selebar bahu atau sedikit lebih lebar
3. Tarik bahu ke belakang dan ke bawah (retract scapula)
4. Turunkan barbell ke bagian bawah dada (sekitar garis puting)
5. Siku membentuk sudut 45° terhadap tubuh (bukan 90°!)
6. Dorong barbell ke atas dengan eksplosif

COMMON MISTAKES:
- Siku terlalu melebar (90°) - berisiko cedera bahu
- Punggung berlebihan arching
- Barbell tidak menyentuh dada
- Mengangkat glutes dari bangku

ALTERNATIVES:
- Dumbbell Press (more ROM)
- Incline Press (upper chest focus)
- Decline Press (lower chest focus)
- Machine Press (more stable)

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.3 DEADLIFT (Conventional Deadlift)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Category: Full Body / Compound Movement
Primary Muscles: Hamstrings, Glutes, Lower Back, Core, Traps, Forearms
Equipment: Barbell, Plates
Difficulty: Advanced (requires proper coaching)

PROPER FORM:
1. Posisi kaki selebar pinggul, shin menyentuh barbell
2. Pegang barbell tepat di luar kaki (grip: overhand atau mixed)
3. Turunkan pinggul, jaga punggung lurus (neutral spine)
4. Dada tegak, bahu sedikit di depan barbell
5. Angkat barbell dengan mendorong lantai (leg drive)
6. Hips dan shoulders naik bersamaan
7. Pada posisi atas, glutes squeezed, shoulders back

CRITICAL SAFETY:
- NEVER round your lower back!
- Hips should not rise faster than shoulders
- Keep barbell close to your body throughout movement

-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
1.4 PULL-UP / CHIN-UP
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Category: Upper Body / Pull Movement
Primary Muscles: Lats, Biceps, Rhomboids, Teres Major
Equipment: Pull-up Bar
Difficulty: Intermediate - Advanced

PROPER FORM:
1. Grip slightly wider than shoulder width (overhand for pull-up)
2. Hang with arms fully extended, shoulders engaged (not shrugged)
3. Pull body up by driving elbows down and back
4. Chin clears the bar at the top
5. Lower with control (3-4 seconds eccentric)

BEGINNER PROGRESSIONS:
- Negative reps (jump up, lower slowly)
- Band-assisted pull-ups
- Lat pulldown machine
- Australian rows (inverted rows)

2.1 FULL BODY PROGRAM (BEGINNER)
-------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Frequency: 3x per week (Mon, Wed, Fri)
Duration: 8-12 weeks
Goal: General fitness, foundational strength

WORKOUT A:
Exercise                      Sets x Reps      Rest
Squat                         3 x 10-12        60s
Bench Press                   3 x 10-12        60s
Bent Over Row                 3 x 10-12        60s
Dumbbell Shoulder Press       3 x 10-12        60s
Plank                         3 x 30-45s       45s

WORKOUT B:
Exercise                      Sets x Reps      Rest
Deadlift (RDL variant)       3 x 10-12        60s
Incline Dumbbell Press        3 x 10-12        60s
Lat Pulldown                  3 x 10-12        60s
Leg Press                     3 x 12-15        60s
Face Pull                     3 x 15-20        45s

PROGRESSION:
- Week 1-4: Focus on form mastery
- Week 5-8: Add weight incrementally
- Week 9-12: Increase intensity
