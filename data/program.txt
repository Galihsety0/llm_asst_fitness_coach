PROGRAMS BY GOAL

WEIGHT LOSS PROGRAM
- Calories: TDEE - 400 (deficit)
- Macros: Protein 2.0g/kg, Carbs 3-5g/kg, Fat 0.8g/kg
- Workout: 4x per week (strength + cardio)
- Monday: Full Body Strength + 15min LISS
- Tuesday: 30-40min LISS
- Wednesday: Full Body Strength + 15min LISS
- Thursday: 30-40min LISS
- Friday: Full Body Strength + 15min LISS
- Saturday: 40-60min LISS (active recovery)
- Sunday: Rest
- Key: Protein at every meal, 7-8 hours sleep, 8,000-10,000 steps/day

MUSCLE GAIN PROGRAM
- Calories: TDEE + 250 (surplus)
- Macros: Protein 1.6-2.2g/kg, Carbs 4-6g/kg, Fat 0.8g/kg
- Workout: 5-6x per week (Push/Pull/Legs or Upper/Lower)
- Monday: Push Day
- Tuesday: Pull Day
- Wednesday: Legs Day
- Thursday: Rest
- Friday: Push Day
- Saturday: Pull Day
- Sunday: Rest
- Key: Progressive overload, track weights, 8+ hours sleep, eat surplus

MAINTENANCE PROGRAM
- Calories: TDEE ± 100
- Macros: Protein 1.6g/kg, Carbs 3-5g/kg, Fat 0.8-1.0g/kg
- Workout: 3-4x per week (enjoyable activities)
- Focus: Consistency, sustainability, enjoyment
- Key: Don't overcomplicate, find what works for you

PERFORMANCE PROGRAM
- Calories: TDEE + 100-200
- Macros: Protein 1.6-2.2g/kg, Carbs 5-8g/kg, Fat 0.8-1.2g/kg
- Workout: Sport-specific periodization
- Focus: Strength, speed, agility, endurance
- Key: Recovery optimization, sleep 9+ hours, active recovery
